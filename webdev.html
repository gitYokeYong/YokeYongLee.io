Shopping Cart



Lists

In conceptualizing an online store, it typically comprises three integral components:

  • product listing
  • the cart
  • the checkout.

Product listing

  • Begin with the product listing
  • Utilize arrays to aggregate these objects.
  • Each product entry should enable addition to the cart.

Shopping Cart

  • Moving to the shopping cart, its functionality is multifaceted.
  • Once populated with products, functionalities like quantity adjustment,
  • removal of items, and displaying individual product totals become essential.
  • The cart should dynamically update as quantities change.

    • Checkout Process

    • consider the checkout process.
    • envision a cash-only scenario, akin to a street market.
    • checkout module should display the final total for all products in the cart.
